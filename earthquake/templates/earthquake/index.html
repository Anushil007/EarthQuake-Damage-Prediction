About Earthquake Building Damage Prediction

The project "Earthquake Damage Prediction" is used to predict the damage grade of buildings damaged by earthquakes. The user can give two modes of input. First, a normal user can just enter his Building ID and find what damage grade of the house is. Secondly, a professional user can input details of 16 columns to get the prediction of damaged grade.
